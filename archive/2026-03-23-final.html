Usage Strategy: Pro vs Max Decision
14:20

Strategy Analyzed real usage data to decide between the $100 Pro and $200 Max plans, then mapped out a smarter way to work going forward.

  • Data: 96% Opus usage, 2,334 prompts in March alone (20x Feb), 6 of 18 tracked days over 2,000 messages
  • Never hit rate limits on Max. Dropping to Pro would force mostly Sonnet and likely cause throttling
  • Decision: stay on Max but restructure how models are used
  • New framework: Tier 1 (Sonnet) for daily execution, Tier 2 (Opus) for judgment calls, Tier 3 (Haiku/Sonnet) for agent workers
  • Default model should be Sonnet. Use /model opus deliberately like a gear shift
  • Skills and hooks are the real multiplier. More encoded patterns = less raw intelligence needed per session
  • Shorter, goal-oriented sessions beat marathon 3,000-message sessions
